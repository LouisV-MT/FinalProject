Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What types of coffee do you serve?

We offer a variety of coffee drinks, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, cold brew, and more. We also serve non-coffee beverages such as teas, smoothies, and fresh juices.

2. Do you offer any vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes! We have plant-based milk alternatives like almond, oat, and soy milk. We also offer a range of dairy-free pastries and snacks.

3. Can I order coffee online or for delivery?

Yes, we offer online ordering for pick-up and delivery through our website and partner apps. You can place your order and choose your preferred method of collection or delivery.

4. Do you have free Wi-Fi?

Yes! We offer complimentary Wi-Fi for all our customers. Feel free to bring your laptop or just enjoy a relaxing time while sipping your coffee.

5. What are your hours of operation?

We are open every day from 7 AM to 8 PM. On weekends, we open earlier at 8 AM and close at 9 PM, so you can enjoy your coffee even later!
